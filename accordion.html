What is artificial intelligence (AI)?
Artificial intelligence, or AI, is the simulation of human intelligence processes by computer systems. It involves the development of algorithms and computer programs that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as visual perception, speech recognition, decision-making, and language translation.
What is the Internet of Things (IoT)?
The Internet of Things (IoT) is a network of physical devices, vehicles, home appliances, and other items that are connected to the internet and can exchange data. This technology allows devices to communicate with each other and with humans, creating a seamless and interconnected world. Examples of IoT devices include smart home devices, fitness trackers, and industrial sensors.
How does blockchain technology work?
Blockchain technology is a decentralized, distributed ledger system that allows digital information to be shared and verified without the need for a central authority. It works by creating a chain of blocks that contain information about transactions, which are validated by a network of computers. Each block in the chain contains a unique code, or "hash," that is used to verify the integrity of the data.
